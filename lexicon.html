  • Babyface

    The good guy. The honorable and ethical hero who defend the helpless and fights cleanly. Ex: john cena, hulk hogan(WWF days), bayley, Sting

  • heel

    The bad guy. The evil person who will break the rules and be disrepectful towards fans. Ex: Brock Lesnar, Randy Orton, Hollywood hulk hogan

  • jobber

    Someone who is booked to lose all the time. Thier job is to make the wrestlers strong

  • Pop

    a loud positive response from the fans

  • Heat

    a negative reaction from the fans. Usually towards the heel. Sometimes they can have x-pac heat, whtich means that they are hated just because of poor ring skills, poor character, repitive story,

  • Promo

    a monologue delivered by a wrestler to promote a story

  • Sell

    making a move look impactful or painful

  • Push

    when a wrestler begins advancing within a promotion by winning more matches and more tv time.

  • Bury

    the act of losing repeatedly to the point you are no longer a threat.

  • Kayfabe

    staying in character in and outsclasse the show

  • Over

    A wrestler who elicits a strong response towards the fans wheter heat or Pop

  • Swerve

    storyline twist. A wrestler turning on his tag team partner
