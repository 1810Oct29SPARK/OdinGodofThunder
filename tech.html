Early wrestling

It all started int eh 1800s when a local carnival strong man would begin challenging locals to matches that would aways end up in his favor because the matches would be fixed in his favor. Realizing that they could make more money off the crowd than entry fees, they began taking wagers on the fight and even having challengers in on the fix, using fake names and hyping the fight.

Creation of NWA

Image source: The WEB

After years of many promotions with many championships with little significance, Promotors created the NWA(the national wrestling association that featured one single championship.

Although the NWA split into different Regions after World war II, they still kept thier gentlemens agreement. In that agreement, they would not steal each others talent or move shows into each others territory
